Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my data usage? +

You can check your data usage in several ways:

  • Dial *123# from your MobiComm number and select the data usage option
  • Download and use the MobiComm mobile app
  • Log in to your account on our website
  • Send an SMS with "DATA" to 1234

Your current billing cycle's data usage is updated approximately every 3-4 hours.

How do I pay my bill online? +

Paying your MobiComm bill online is easy and convenient. Here's how:

  1. Visit our website and log in to your account
  2. Navigate to the "Billing" or "Payments" section
  3. Select your preferred payment method (credit/debit card, net banking, UPI, etc.)
  4. Enter the required payment details
  5. Confirm your payment

You can also set up automatic payments to avoid late fees and service interruptions.

How do I upgrade my plan? +

To upgrade your MobiComm plan, you can:

  • Log in to your account on our website and navigate to the "Plans" section
  • Use the MobiComm mobile app and select "Change Plan" option
  • Visit any MobiComm service center with a valid ID proof
  • Call our customer support at 1800-123-456 to request a plan upgrade

Plan changes typically take effect within 24 hours or at the start of your next billing cycle, depending on the type of plan.

What should I do if my device is not connecting to the network? +

If your device is not connecting to the network, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Toggle Airplane mode on and off
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check if your SIM card is properly inserted
  4. Ensure your account is active and bills are up-to-date
  5. Check for network outages in your area on our website or app
  6. Try manually selecting the network in your device settings

If none of these steps work, please contact our customer support for further assistance.

How do I activate international roaming? +

To activate international roaming on your MobiComm connection:

  • Log in to your MobiComm account and navigate to the "Services" section
  • Select "International Roaming" and choose your destination country
  • Select a suitable roaming package based on your travel duration and needs
  • Complete the payment for the selected package
  • Roaming services will be activated within 2 hours

Alternatively, you can call our customer service at 1800-123-456 or visit any MobiComm service center to activate international roaming.
